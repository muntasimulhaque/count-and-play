Privacy Policy — Count & Play

Last updated: July 13, 2026

Count & Play is a simple educational app that helps young children learn addition and subtraction by counting fruits and other objects. It is designed for children and their parents.

Data we collect

None. Count & Play does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information or any other data. There are no accounts, no analytics, no advertising, no in-app purchases, and no third-party SDKs of any kind.

How the app works

The app is fully self-contained. The counting voice is generated on your device by its built-in text-to-speech engine; no audio or text ever leaves the device. After the first load, the app can work entirely offline.

Children's privacy

Because the app collects no data at all, no data is collected from children. The app complies with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA), the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), and Google Play's Families policies.

Changes to this policy

If the app's data practices ever change, this page will be updated before any new version is released.
